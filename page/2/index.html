What is Hexo

Hexo is a blog engine template that enable you to create your blog quickly. I try to use easy understanding words to introduce it. If you are familiar with it or have experience with other engine such as Jeklly, you can just skip over it:)
The following chapter is to tell you how to beign with Hexo in Local machine. To see how to put it in the github or internet with own URL name, please refer to my articles (not finished yet).
